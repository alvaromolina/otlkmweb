This is a preventive action it has had to take to protect its customers while investigations are carried out and appropriate measures are taken. As a result, customers may have experienced a slowdown of their services and interfaces.

  • DRP – Disaster Recovery Plan: This is translated as Disaster Recovery Plan, DRP. This is known as a set of actions and human, technical and procedural resources that serve to recover, in the minimum time and at the lowest cost, an interrupted activity within a company due to an emergency.
